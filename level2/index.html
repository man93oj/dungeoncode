Level 2: CONDITIONS AND LOOPS

Conditions and loop allow you to automate and control how many times you want a certain block of code to run.

For example if you wanted to count how many jelly beans are in a jar, you would tell the computer to keep counting jelly beans until every jelly bean has in the jar has been counted. The CONDITION is "until every jelly bean has been counted" and the LOOP is to "count" over and over until the CONDITION is met.
